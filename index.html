WELCOME  TO  COMPUPERFECT ENGINEERING LIMITED

COMPUPERFECT ENGINEERING LTD. is an ICT firm dynamically positioned to offer an end to end service. A repair depot specializing in electronics, PC board repair, industrial electronics/instrumentation repair and control systems.

In today's competitive information age, we as a matter of policy, regularly apply and engage suitable technology to meet human need that could justify the Return On Investment by our clients. Our mission is to work in collaboration with your in-house technical personnel to see to the smooth running of your equipment/machinery in other to minimize downtimes.

At the point of justification, we have many years of quality customer service as a provider of guaranteed electronic component repairs and re-manufacture of electronic board. We give holistic consultation about what is on ground thus, using state of the art equipment to troubleshoot problems down to the component level, which often enables us to provide repairs at a cost lower than those of replacement parts.
